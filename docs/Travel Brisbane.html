Discover Brisbane During AusDM 2025

Make the most of your visit to Brisbane by exploring its world-class attractions. From riverside walks and outdoor adventures to buzzing dining precincts, the city has something for everyone. All destinations are conveniently accessible via public transport or the CityCat ferry service that runs along the Brisbane River.

South Bank

A vibrant cultural and recreational precinct just a short walk from QUT Gardens Point. South Bank features lush parklands, riverside dining, the iconic Streets Beach, and world-class galleries. Whether you're after a relaxing afternoon or a lively evening by the river, it’s a must-visit destination in Brisbane.

Queen’s Wharf

Brisbane’s newest entertainment hub, Queen’s Wharf features luxury shopping, vibrant dining, and a riverside promenade. When complete, it will offer sky deck views and dynamic public spaces. Located a stone’s throw from the QUT campus, it’s a glimpse into Brisbane’s urban future.

Kangaroo Point

Arguably the most stunning spot to enjoy Brisbane’s sunset and skyline views. Just across from QUT’s Gardens Point campus, it’s perfect for a relaxing stroll—or, if you’re feeling adventurous, try rock climbing on the iconic cliffs.

Story Bridge

One of Brisbane’s most iconic landmarks, the Story Bridge offers panoramic views of the city and river. For thrill-seekers, the bridge climb provides a unique adventure above the skyline. At night, the bridge comes alive with spectacular lighting—perfect for photos and evening strolls along the riverwalk.

Howard Smith Wharves

Tucked beneath the Story Bridge, this lively riverside precinct blends heritage charm with modern flair. Enjoy award-winning restaurants, craft breweries, and scenic views of the Brisbane River. It’s an ideal place to unwind after a day of conference activities.

CityCat Ferry

Enjoy a scenic and effortless journey along the Brisbane River with the CityCat — Brisbane’s iconic river ferry. With frequent stops near major attractions, it’s a relaxing and practical way to explore the city. You might even catch the world’s only Bluey-themed ferry! Best of all, smart ticketing means you can tap on with your credit card — often for as little as 50 cents.
